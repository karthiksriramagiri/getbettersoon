Why Do I Need Glasses?

Recent 3 min read

Glasses Help Your Eyes See Clearly

Glasses help your eyes see clearly. Sometimes your eyes have a hard time focusing, and that can make things look different than they really are!

If you have difficulty seeing, things can look fuzzy or blurry, and you might need to squint to see things properly!

When you visit the eye doctor, you can take an eye test where you can read fun shapes and letters to test your vision!

Glasses work by helping focus the light that your eyes are having trouble doing. This creates a clear image for you!

Be Confident with Your Glasses!

You can try on different kinds of glasses and see which one suits you the best! Plenty of kids and adults have vision issues, so be fun and be confident with your new glasses!

Remember

Glasses are just a tool to help you see the world more clearly. You're still the amazing person you've always been!
