About Me

Hi, I'm Sriprada! A premed student passionate about making healthcare accessible for families

Hello, I'm Sriprada Rayavarapu

Premed Student | Aspiring Cardiologist | Health Education Advocate

As a premed student with dreams of attending medical school, I've always been passionate about making healthcare knowledge accessible to everyone - especially families with young children.

GetBetterSoon was born from my own experiences seeing how scared and confused children can feel when facing medical situations or health challenges. I believe that when families understand what's happening, everyone feels more confident and less afraid.

Through my premed studies and research, I've learned that education is one of the most powerful tools in healthcare. My goal is to bridge the gap between complex medical information and family-friendly understanding.

Studying to make healthcare more accessible for everyone 📚✨

Why GetBetterSoon?

During my premed studies, I noticed a significant gap between medical knowledge and public understanding, especially when it comes to children's health. Parents often feel overwhelmed by medical jargon, and children feel scared by the unknown.

GetBetterSoon is my way of using my medical education to create a bridge. Every article is:

  • 📚 Research-Based: Grounded in my medical studies and current healthcare research
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family-Friendly: Written in language that both kids and parents can understand
  • 💕 Empathetic: Created with understanding of the fears and concerns families face
  • 🔬 Accurate: Reviewed and fact-checked using my medical knowledge and reliable sources
